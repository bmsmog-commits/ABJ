Common questions

Everything you need to know about ABJ Foundation.

You can donate securely through our donation page. Select the amount, currency and payment method, then complete the payment through one of our trusted gateway partners.

Individuals and families facing hardship in the communities we serve may request support. We review each request carefully to ensure assistance reaches those most in need.

We support Stripe, PayPal, Flutterwave, Paystack, and bank transfer. For bank transfer, we will share the account details with you after your donation request is submitted.

Verification typically takes 3-5 business days. Our team may contact you for additional information to ensure your request is processed quickly and fairly.

Visit the About page to learn about volunteering opportunities and contact our team if you want to support our programs in the field or remotely.

We work across multiple regions globally with a focus on supporting vulnerable communities in Africa, Asia, and Latin America through local partnerships and projects.
