Need Assistance?

If you or your family need help, ABJ Foundation is ready to support you. We provide emergency relief, medical aid, education support, and essential supplies.

Emergency Relief

Immediate support for families in crisis through food, shelter and cash assistance.

Medical Aid

Healthcare access for urgent treatment, medication, and hospital support.

Education Support

Scholarships, school fees and learning materials for children.

How It Works

  1. Tell Us Your Need
    Use our help request form to describe your situation clearly.
  2. We Review Your Case
    Our team contacts you to verify your request and offer support.
  3. Receive Assistance
    We deliver aid as quickly as possible based on availability.

If you already have an account, sign in to track your request status from the dashboard.
